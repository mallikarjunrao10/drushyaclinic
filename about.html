Meet Our Founder

Dr. V. Suman Chandar Rao

A Leader in Skin & Hair Care

Dr. V. Suman Chandar Rao founded Drushya Skin, Laser & Hair Transplant Center with a vision to provide the highest quality of care in skin, hair, and laser treatments. As a distinguished Dermatologist, Cosmetologist, and Dermato Surgeon, he is widely recognized as the top hair transplant specialist in Karimnagar.

Holding an M.D. (DVL) from Osmania Medical College, he offers advanced procedures like Bio-FUE Hair Transplant, alongside comprehensive solutions for conditions like V.D. leprosy and allergies, ensuring every patient receives dedicated and personalized treatment.

Specializations & Expertise:

  • Dermatology & Venereology
  • Hair Transplant & Restoration
  • Skin, V.D. Leprosy & Allergy Specialist
  • Cosmetic & Aesthetic Treatments
  • Dermato Surgery
  • Laser Treatments
