Our Specialized Hair Transplant Techniques

Bio-FUE Hair Transplant

A cutting-edge modification of the standard FUE technique. It involves enriching the scalp environment with **Bio-enhanced growth factors (like PRP)** before and after implantation. This boosts graft survival, accelerates healing, and promotes thicker growth.

Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)

The gold standard procedure where individual follicular units are delicately extracted using specialized micro-punches and then transplanted. This technique is preferred for its **scarless healing** in the donor area and superior natural look.

Density & Hairline Design

The success of a transplant lies in the design. We focus on creating a **natural, age-appropriate hairline** and achieving high density in balding areas by meticulously placing each graft at the correct angle and direction.

Are You a Candidate for Hair Transplant?

A hair transplant can be life-changing for men and women experiencing:

  • Male/Female Pattern Baldness (Androgenetic Alopecia): The most common reason for hair loss.
  • Hair loss due to scarring from burns or injuries.
  • Thinning Eyebrows or Beard (transplants can also address these areas).

The key requirement is a sufficient supply of healthy donor hair. A one-on-one consultation is the only way to accurately assess your candidacy and outline a precise treatment plan tailored to your expectations.
